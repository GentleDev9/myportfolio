Why We Love Cats

  • Thier beauty is unique-every cat looks and acts differently.

Fun Facts About Cats

  • Cats can sleep for 12-16 hours a day.
  • They can jump up to six times thier body length in one leap.
  • Cats use thier whiskers to sense their surroundings
  • A cat's purr is believed to reduce stress and even help with healing
  • Each cats has a unique "meow" to communicate with humans

Cats Behaviours and Personalities

cats are famous for being mysterious,but they have clear ways of showing feelings:

  • purring - A sign of happiness and comfort
  • Kneading - Pressing paws on soft surfaces,which shows affection
  • Tail movement - Astraight-up tail means they're happy; a flicking tail can mean they are annoyed
  • Curiousity - Cats love exploring new places,no matter how small

Caring for Cat

to keep a cat happy and healthy:

  • Provide fresh foods and clean water daily
  • Give them cozy,safe spaces to rest
  • Play with them often to keep them active
  • Take them to the vets of check-ups
  • Show love and affection,but also respect their space
